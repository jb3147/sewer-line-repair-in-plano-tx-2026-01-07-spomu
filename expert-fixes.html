What is Expert Fixes?

Expert Fixes represents the cutting edge of sewer line repair technology. Instead of traditional excavation that tears up your yard, driveway, and landscaping, we use advanced trenchless methods combined with high-definition camera inspection to diagnose and repair your sewer line with surgical precision. This approach solves the same problem—broken, cracked, or failing sewer lines—but with dramatically less disruption to your property and often at a lower cost.

Our state-of-the-art cameras allow us to see exactly what's wrong inside your pipes before we make a single repair. This means no guesswork, no surprises, and no unnecessary digging. Whether your line has tree root intrusion, cracks, belly sections, or blockages, we identify the root cause and apply the most effective solution.

Who It's Ideal For

Expert Fixes is perfect for homeowners and business owners in Plano who want their sewer line problems solved without the nightmare of major excavation. If you're concerned about your driveway, landscaping, pool, or underground utilities, or if you simply want the fastest, most efficient repair possible, this is your solution. It's also ideal for older homes where traditional digging could compromise the foundation or structure.

The Problem It Solves

Sewer line problems don't just disappear—they get worse. Slow drains, backups, foul odors, and wet patches in your yard are warning signs of a failing sewer line. Traditional repair methods require tearing up your property, taking weeks to complete, and leaving you with massive restoration costs. Expert Fixes eliminates this dilemma by providing a faster, cleaner, and often more affordable repair method that gets your plumbing flowing again without destroying your landscape.

How Expert Fixes Works

A simple, transparent process from diagnosis to completion

1

Initial Inspection & Assessment

We arrive at your Plano property and perform a thorough initial assessment. We listen to your concerns, check for visible signs of sewer problems, and discuss your situation. This helps us understand the scope before we deploy our camera equipment.

2

HD Camera Inspection

We insert our state-of-the-art HD camera into your sewer line to see exactly what's happening inside. The camera footage shows us cracks, root intrusion, belly sections, blockages, and any other damage. We provide you with a detailed report and video evidence of the problem.

3

Customized Repair Plan & Quote

Based on the camera inspection, we recommend the best repair method for your specific situation. We explain your options—whether it's pipe bursting, pipe lining, or traditional repair—and provide a transparent, itemized quote with no hidden fees.

4

Expert Repair Execution

Once you approve, our licensed technicians execute the repair using trenchless technology. We work efficiently with minimal disruption to your property. Most repairs are completed within 1-2 days. We keep your property clean and leave the work area spotless.

5

Final Inspection & Warranty

We perform a final camera inspection to confirm the repair is complete and your sewer line is flowing perfectly. We provide you with documentation and our warranty guarantee. Your plumbing is now back to normal, and you have peace of mind.
